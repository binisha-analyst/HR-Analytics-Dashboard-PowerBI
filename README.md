# Project Overview
This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze workforce trends, employee distribution, and attrition patterns.
The dashboard enables HR managers and leadership teams to monitor key employee metrics and make data-driven retention decisions.

# Business Objective
To analyze employee data and identify patterns related to:
- Employee Attrition
- Department-wise workforce distribution
- Salary trends
- Age and experience breakdown
- Job role performance
# Key KPIs Monitored
- Total Employees
- Attrition Rate (%)
- Average Salary
- Average Tenure
- Department-wise Attrition
- Gender Distribution
# Key Insights
- The organization has a total of **311** employees, with an attrition rate of **33%**, indicating moderate employee turnover.
- **67%** of employees are currently active, while **104** employees have left the organization.
- The workforce consists of **57%** female and **43%** male employees, showing relatively balanced gender representation.
- The **Production department** has the highest number of employees compared to other departments.
- The majority of employees fall under the “Fully Meets” performance rating **(78%)**, indicating strong overall performance levels.
- Most employees report High to Very High satisfaction levels, suggesting generally positive workplace engagement.
- **Indeed and LinkedIn** are the top recruitment sources contributing the highest number of hires.
- The total salary expenditure is approximately **21.47M**, with Production contributing the largest portion of salary allocation.
